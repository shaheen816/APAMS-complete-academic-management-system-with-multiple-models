# APAMS (Afghanistan Police Academy Management System) v1.0 👮‍♂️🇦🇫

APAMS is a dedicated digital ecosystem designed to transform a civilian applicant into a commissioned police officer. Unlike generic university systems, APAMS manages every tactical, academic, disciplinary, and financial record within a secure, on-premises environment.

## 🏛 The 12 Pillars (Core Modules)
The system is built on 12 integrated functional modules:
1.  **Admissions & Recruitment:** PFT results, medical exams, and merit lists.
2.  **Curriculum Management:** Full academic program and syllabus repository.
3.  **Class & Timetable:** Conflict-free scheduling for drills and weapon training.
4.  **Examination & Grading:** Automated GPA/CGPA and transcript generation.
5.  **Library & Resources:** Cataloging and digital resource management.
6.  **Cadet/Officer Master File:** A 360-degree secure digital bio-data file.
7.  **Attendance & Field Training:** Daily class/parade tracking and leave workflows.
8.  **Discipline & Conduct:** Incident reporting and disciplinary inquiry workflows.
9.  **Human Resources:** Staff service history and promotion management.
10. **Finance & Accounts:** Fee collection, payroll prep, and procurement.
11. **Inventory & Logistics:** Armory sub-module (weapons/ammo) and fleet tracking.
12. **Commander’s Dashboard:** Real-time KPIs and system-wide audit logs.

## 🚀 Technical Architecture
Designed for **Maximum Performance** on a Local Area Network (LAN):
*   **Frontend:** Pure PHP (Standardizing for high-speed server-side rendering).
*   **Backend Database:** MySQL 8.x (InnoDB) for ACID compliance.
*   **Architecture:** Three-Tier B/S Architecture optimized for LAN.
*   **Security:** Strict Military-style Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

## 📅 Roadmap & Iterative Delivery
We are delivering APAMS in 4 major increments:
*   **Iteration 1 (Weeks 1-8):** Core Foundation & Master Data Structure.
*   **Iteration 2 (Weeks 9-18):** The Academic Engine (Admissions & Exams).
*   **Iteration 3 (Weeks 19-27):** The Operational Hub (Attendance & Discipline).
*   **Iteration 4 (Weeks 28-34):** Admin, Finance & Final Integration.

## 🛠 Installation (LAN Environment)
1. **Server Requirements:** 8-core Xeon, 64GB RAM (App & DB Servers).
2. **Database:** Import `apams_master_schema.sql` into MySQL 8.x.
3. **Environment:** Ensure the server is running on the Academy's secure LAN (Internet-free).
